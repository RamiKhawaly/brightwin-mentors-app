**Brightwin** is a mentorship and career growth app by **Brightway** that connects **job seekers** and **mentors** in the tech industry.
Target Audience:
Job Seekers are STEM engineers or students who are looking for their first job or to upgrade their existing job

Mentors can be STEM Engineers, Managers, or HR who work in a company with a referral policy.

Mentors can:

* Post internal jobs and earn referral bonuses
* Offer guidance through chats, calls, or mock interviews
* Give feedback and earn badges for engagement



Project structure:
backend - backend code
mentors-app - code for the mentors application
users-app - code for the job seekers app
admin-dashboard - code for the administration panel
